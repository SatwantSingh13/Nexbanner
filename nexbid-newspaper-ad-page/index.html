300x250 video-first monetization

NexBanner player for GAM banner placements.

This demo shows how a normal 300x250 Google Ad Manager creative can load the NexBanner player, try VAST video demand first, and fall back to display or remnant ORTB demand in the same slot.

300x250 GAM creative simulation
1. GAM CreativeGAM serves one NexBanner script into the 300x250 slot.
2. Video FirstThe mini player checks premium VAST demand and plays muted inline video.
3. Display FallbackIf VAST fails, the same slot renders display demand.
4. ORTB BackfillIf display fails, the final NexBanner AdX/remnant endpoint can bid.
